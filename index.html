About Us

TSAPO Industries Zambia is the official supplier of four main brands; FAW Trucks, Sino Trucks, Lovol Mining and Construction Equipment and the Golden Dragon buses. These renowned brands offer state-of-the-art commercial products which are reliable, affordable and suitable for the African terrains. We provide our customers with genuine products from the official OEMs which are backed by warranty to ensure value deliverance through product support and guaranteeing total customer satisfaction. We provide excellent aftersales through our modernized workshops, mobile support services and warranty services.

All Vehicles are covered by a comprehensive warranty at industry standard, ensuring customers have complete peace of mind on their purchase. We process all warranty claims on behalf of our customers and in some instances, we perform the warranty job while the approval process is still in progress. The gesture is meant to ensure that vehicles are always moving and that our clients have the peace of mind.
